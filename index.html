Research concept

Smart Pollination

A smart research platform for date palm pollination. It combines AI image analysis with field tools to help identify the right pollination window and support faster decisions in the farm.

Main research tracks
Mobile application Capture palm flower images, analyze readiness, and provide a clear recommendation to the farmer.
Integration with robots Connect analysis results with robotic pollination systems for precise field execution.
Integration with drones Use drones for farm scanning, location tracking, and monitoring pollination status across palm rows.
From image diagnosis to smart field action

The project connects mobile inspection, robotic execution, and drone monitoring in one practical workflow.
